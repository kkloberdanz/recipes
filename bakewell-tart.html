Bakewell Tart

Ingredients

Crust

  • 8 tablespoons butter
  • 3/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 1 3/4 cup flour
  • 1 pinch salt

Custard Filling

  • 5 egg yolks
  • 1 egg white
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 8 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1/4 cup almond flour

Jam Filling

  • 1 1/2 cup raspberry jam

Instructions

Crust

  1. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the 8 tablespoons of cold butter and sugar until it forms a nice fluffy paste.
  2. Add the 3 egg yolks, and beat until it looks smooth.
  3. Sift in the flour and incorporate slowly until the dough looks crumbly.
  4. Turn dough out onto parchment.
  5. Place another sheet of parchment on top of the dough.
  6. Use a rolling pin to flatten the dough out into a thin disc.
  7. Chill in the fridge for 30 minutes.
  8. Line a greased pie tin with the dough.
  9. Place the pie tin (with dough) in the freezer for 30 minutes.
  10. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
  11. Place pie weights on top of the dough.
  12. Bake for 15 minutes.
  13. Remove weights and bake until edges are golden brown (about 5 to 10 minutes).
  14. Remove from oven and let it cool completely.

Custard Filling

  1. Heat oven to 325 degrees F.
  2. Beat the 5 egg yolks and 1 egg white in a large mixing bowl until smooth.
  3. Mix in sugar and melted butter until smooth.
  4. Mix in almond flour until smooth.

Putting it all together

  1. Allow the pie crust to fully cool.
  2. Spread jam on pie crust.
  3. Slowly pour custard on top of the jam layer.
  4. (Optional) Decorate top with almond flakes.
  5. Bake in oven for 35 to 40 minutes until custard has set.
